How to Play

  1. Click Start Camera and allow camera access.
  2. Place your hand inside the dashed box on the video feed.
  3. Show a clear gesture: Rock (fist), Paper (open palm), or Scissors (V sign).
  4. Click Capture to get a prediction, or use Game Mode for a fun match against the computer!
  5. For best results, use good lighting and keep your hand centered.

Enjoy playing and testing the AI!
